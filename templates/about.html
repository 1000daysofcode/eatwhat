What is EatWhat?

EatWhat is a unique, yet simple solution to the simple and hard question: what do I eat?

Pop in some basic information, such as some food restrictions and a few favorite flavors.

Confirm your location, wait time and different country cuisines you're open to and you're done!

Our current version is in pre-alpha and does not yet save preferences or host a large database.
